Training

  • Obedience
    We offer 3 different levels of obedience traing; Intro to Obedience, Basic Obedience, and Advanced Obedience. The Intro course is designed for puppies and new owner to learn and begin working and build that needed relationship with the dog. Basic covers all basic commands like sit, stay, down, and begins to introduce more advance techniques like place and heel. Our advance course offers an owner the chance to really test the dogs full potentional! After completion of this course your dog will be able to hold a place, heel off leash around a busy enviroment, and much much more!
  • Service Dog
    We offer a longer course after advanced obedience that registers your dog as a service dog. The timeline will vary depending on the dogs performance but its estimated completion time is 16-32 weeks. Other stipulations to this you must provide us with a Physicians note stating clinically why and what services your dog needs to be trained for. (Also not all dog breeds are eligable for this training due to state and government restrictions.)
  • Tactical
    We work with the local police, sheriff, and other law enforcement agencies to help them achieve whatever mission the need. Whether you need a drug and bomb sniffing K9, a multipurpose K9, or a bite dog we can assist in reaching those goals. We also offer home defense K9 training for the civilian sector for those who want the best gaurd dog around!

Boarding

  • Daily Boarding
    We offer daily boarding for dogs Monday through Friday with dropoffs beginning at 0630 and pickup at 1900. Each boarding give 3-5 potty breaks and 30 minutes of playtime in our K9 play pen with other dogs! We offer monthly memberships and a discounted annual if you want to go that route.
  • Weekend
    We also offer a weekend or trip style boarding for the once in awhile type of trips. If your already a member you will recieve a 10% discount at the end of your dogs stay. Each boarding give 3-5 potty breaks and 30 minutes of playtime in our K9 play pen with other dogs!
  • Extended Stay
    We understand that family emergencies, work, and even military deployments happen. We also know the bond between man and dog is nearly inseperable. This type of boarding allows you to be able to go on those long trips, emergencies, and work associated things without having to give up your dog to a foster or a shelter. Any stay over 2 weeks will fall into this catagory. Prices may vary depending on circumstance and time stay.
